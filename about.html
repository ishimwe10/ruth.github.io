more about networking

Networking, or computer networking, is the process of connecting
two or more computing devices, such as desktop computers, mobile devices,
routers or applications, to enable the transmission and exchange of information and resources

Networked devices rely on communications protocols—rules that describe
how to transmit or exchange data across a network—to share information over physical or wireless connections.
